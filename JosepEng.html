Next Project, a beginning and an end

Why did I start these studies?

Own idea to develop, unification of the tattoo sector.
Nowadays, when a person decides to get a tattoo, after a long time of decision, it is usually difficult to find a person who has a design style suitable to their preferences, unless they already have a studio or references from third persons. This sector even has some difficulties in making itself known publicly, and in the city of Barcelona the only way they have to make themselves heard is by advertising in the studio or nearby by means of fliers. Studying in the centre, I noticed and even saw several studios where the workers seemed doing nothing.

My idea is to create an application where the different studios can be unified in the same place, classifying them by styles, services, etc. and that the users of the application have this where they can find information about where, when and how much the service they require costs. At the same time, these studios would be paying a small charge to make themselves known to a much wider range than they currently do. Also, taking advantage of this portal, it should have a section where people who have just obtained knowledge and are suitable for the job, can access a kind of job offers board, because it is very difficult to find a studio to work in. In this way, this app would include a small portfolio for these designers where they could show their knowledge and the studios would already have a small idea of how that person works and what style of tattoo they have.
